Jamie's Part of the Story

Jamie grew up in San Francisco in a wealthy family.
His mother was a successful lawyer and his father a renowned microbiologist. They always lived in the best neighborhoods in the country and as soon as Jaime turned 18, his parents got him into his dream university and later into a career as a lobbyist through their connections and positions.
After a few years, he decided to write a book about his resounding success stating that "anyone with the will can do what I did". He explained that social justice and its inequalities were for those who still believed in it.
