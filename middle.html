Zuri's Part of the Story

Against Jaime's views was Zuri Kalaney, a publicist for a local agency. She grew up middle class for most of her life, until she left home.
In recent years, she was a single mother trying to support herself and her child while living through the time of price inflation.
Jaime's words have frustrated her immensely, as her greatest motivation in life is to give her son the stability she grew up with, but she finds herself struggling.
She wonders if she will ever be able to buy a house or pay for her child's post-secondary education.
